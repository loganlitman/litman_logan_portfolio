Thermostat Complete Product, Website, & Brand Design. London, ON

In October of 2018, one of the projects assigned to us at Fanshawe College, was to get into groups of four and completely design, brand, and create a website for a thermostat in 8 hours; "ThermoSecure". This project was treated as our midterm.

Me and my group began by designing the thermostat using Cinema 4D, and then created the logo and name. We wanted it to be obvious that our product was a thermostat, so we kept that in mind when creating the name. As we began creating the logo, we wanted to embody comfort, cooling, and migrating several temperatures. To do this, we decided to showcase four seperate triangles in our logo - each meaning different temperatures and comfort levels, and having them connect in the middle.
