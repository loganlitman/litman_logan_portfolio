London Squash & Fitness Club Redesign and Rebrand, London, ON.

In December of 2017, the London Squash & Fitness Club approached Fanshawe, in hopes of the students redesigning and rebranding their current (very outdated) website. This was a team project, so I got together with a partner and we began. This project was two months long, so we wanted to give LSFC a complete makeover.

Our inspiration for this project was to take their white and green site, that looked as if it were designed decades ago, and give it a modern, dark, and sporty feel. For that reason we chose a very dark grey, and the clubs pre-existing gold colour as our primary colour palette. We produced unique images that showcased the intense competitiveness of the club, as well as a sleek, dark website that embodied the same ideas.
