Elite Hockey Prep Camps Logo Design and Graphics, Freelance. Toronto, ON.

As a freelance graphic designer, I am open to almost any project, and am always excited for a new creative task. When a client approached me asking for work regarding a hockey program logo and branding, I was thrilled about the oppurtunity to get involved with something that was so close to home. Growing up with two brothers who played rep hockey, I knew I had a good idea of what the client wanted - and that it would be much different than other freelance work I had previously done (some of which can be seen here).

Working closely with the president of the company has allowed me to garner skills that will be useful in the future with important clients. This project is unique to my other work as the look of the logo is rugged, and not like the rest of my modern, crisp graphics. I am still working with Elite Hockey Prep Camps, helping with other graphics, flyers, and various design elements as requested.
