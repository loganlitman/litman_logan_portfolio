Freelance Club & Promotional Graphics for The Wallen. Toronto, ON.

As a freelance graphic designer, I am excited to take on any project. When The Wallen, a nightclub located in the heart of downtown Toronto, contacted me to be the head graphic designer of their promotional and clubbing graphics, I was excited and ready for the task. My job was to create three posters per week (each sized and designed respectively for Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, & Event Brite). Each night required a unique and creative design & theme.
