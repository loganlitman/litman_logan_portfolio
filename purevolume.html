PureVolume Pop Culture News Outlet Redesign and Rebrand, London, ON.

As a student in the Interactive Media program at Fanshawe College, each year a different client approaches the college in hopes of the students redesigning them something they wont forget. in September of 2018, I got together with some other classmates (Developers) and rebranded and designed PureVolume.

PureVolume is a media news company, (similar to E news or People), that users rely on for their daily dose of pop gossip. Right off the bat, we wanted to showcase something different, and that begins with a different color - one that people don't see used everyday in design. We decided to use tones of purple and have the brand (the logo, video, and everything else that represents it) exhibit soundwaves. Below is what my group members and I came up with.
